What is KEMETION?

+

KEMETION is India's 100% free fantasy cricket platform where you can create virtual cricket teams and compete with other fans based on real match performances. It's completely free to play with no real money involved.

Is KEMETION really free?

+

Yes, KEMETION is 100% free forever. We do not:

  • Charge any entry fees for contests
  • Accept any real money deposits
  • Process any withdrawals
  • Offer any real money prizes

Points earned are for entertainment and leaderboard rankings only.

Is fantasy cricket legal in India?

+

Yes, fantasy sports is legal in most parts of India. The Supreme Court and various High Courts have recognized fantasy sports as games of skill, which are legal under Indian law. However, some states have restrictions, which is why we don't offer services in Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Nagaland, Odisha, Sikkim, and Telangana.

How do I create a fantasy team?

+

Creating a team is simple:

  • Select a match from the Contests page
  • Pick 11 players from both teams
  • Follow the role constraints (WK, BAT, AR, BOWL)
  • Choose your Captain (2x points) and Vice-Captain (1.5x points)
  • Submit your team before the match starts

How are fantasy points calculated?

+

Points are calculated based on real match performance:

  • Batting: Runs, boundaries, centuries, etc.
  • Bowling: Wickets, maiden overs, economy rate
  • Fielding: Catches, stumpings, run outs
  • Captain gets 2x points, Vice-Captain gets 1.5x points

Visit our How to Play page for the complete points system.

Can I edit my team after creating it?

+

Yes, you can edit your team any number of times before the match starts. Once the match begins, no changes are allowed. Make sure to finalize your team based on the playing XI announcement.

What happens if a player I selected doesn't play?

+

If a player you selected doesn't play in the match, they will earn 0 points. There are no automatic substitutions. We recommend checking the playing XI announcement before the match and updating your team accordingly.
