About SDSURABHI

India's 100% Free Fantasy Cricket Platform

Who We Are

SDSURABHI is India's premier free-to-play fantasy cricket platform, operated by SDSURABHI INFRA PRIVATE LIMITED. We are passionate cricket enthusiasts who believe that everyone should have access to the excitement of fantasy sports without any financial barriers.

Founded with a vision to democratize fantasy sports, we offer a completely free platform where cricket fans can showcase their knowledge, compete with friends, and enjoy the thrill of fantasy cricket without spending a single rupee.

Our platform is built on the principles of fair play, transparency, and responsible gaming. We are committed to providing a safe, secure, and enjoyable experience for all our users.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide every cricket fan in India with a free, fair, and fun platform to enjoy fantasy cricket. We aim to make fantasy sports accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation, while promoting responsible gaming practices.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and loved free fantasy sports platform. We envision a community where millions of cricket fans come together to celebrate their passion for the game through skill-based entertainment.
